Tech+Social Impact

A Club that Promotes Collaboration, Equality, and Social Awareness through Technology

Purpose

This club is one that thrives on diversity. The long-term vision for our organization is to create a tight-knit community that harnesses the power of the strong tech culture in Berkeley to develop projects and conversations that are socially relevant. Join us to find out how you can use your programming prowess to do some good in the community!

Take a look at some of our past events!

From a speaker series featuring powerful women in tech to a competition that encourages students to talk with each other and build something that can help the world, our organization has and will continue to do it all. Take a look at some of our past events!

Some of our past social media posts

We have made some interesting posts across the years that call on everyone to take action in whatever way they can and encourage everyone to uphold their civic duties. Let us know what you think!
